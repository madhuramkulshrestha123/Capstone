Dashboard Image Replacement Guide

How to Replace Placeholder Images

Step 1: Prepare Your Images

Create or select images that match the recommended sizes:

  • Carousel Images: 1200x400 pixels (JPG, PNG, or WEBP format)
  • Gallery Images: 600x400 pixels (JPG, PNG, or WEBP format)

Step 2: Rename Your Images

Rename your images to match these exact filenames:

  • carousel1.jpg
  • carousel2.jpg
  • carousel3.jpg
  • carousel4.jpg
  • gallery1.jpg
  • gallery2.jpg
  • gallery3.jpg

Step 3: Replace the Files

Copy your renamed images to this folder, replacing the existing placeholder files:

/public/images/

Your dashboard will automatically use the new images.
